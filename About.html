Our Mission

ResConnects' mission is to empower students with authentic, peer-to-peer reviews and ratings of student accommodations. We provide a trusted platform where students can make informed housing decisions and share their real-life experiences to help others find their perfect home away from home.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every student, wherever they may be, can find a welcoming and secure atmosphere. To guarantee that no student ever has to feel alone or unsure about where they live, we see ResConnects as the beating heart of student housing — a reliable network that not only informs choices but also builds communities.
